# MH-118FQ Quartz Plate / Quartz Microscope Slide

**Product URL:** [muhwa-tech.com/products](https://www.muhwa-tech.com)

## Overview

A high-purity fused quartz plate designed for UV-Vis spectroscopy, fluorescence microscopy, and applications requiring exceptional optical transmission in the ultraviolet range. Unlike standard glass slides, quartz offers transparency down to 190nm.

## Key Advantages

1. **Deep UV Transmission:** Transparent to 190nm — critical for UV spectroscopy of proteins, nucleic acids, and organic compounds
2. **Thermal Stability:** Withstands temperatures far beyond standard glass slides — suitable for high-temperature sample processing
3. **Chemical Inertness:** Resistant to most acids and solvents — won't leach contaminants into samples
4. **Optical Clarity:** Minimal autofluorescence for sensitive fluorescence applications

## Applications

- UV-Vis spectrophotometry
- Fluorescence microscopy (low autofluorescence)
- High-temperature microscopy
- Spectroscopic sample holders
- Semiconductor inspection
