## Overview

A cordless, rechargeable electric pipette controller designed for use with serological pipettes (1mL to 100mL). Replaces manual rubber bulbs and provides precise, fatigue-free aspiration and dispensing for cell culture, media preparation, and general liquid handling.

| Mode | Function | Best For |

## Key Advantages

1. **Eliminates Repetitive Strain:** Electric aspiration replaces manual rubber bulb squeezing — essential for high-throughput labs
2. **Precision Control:** Adjustable speed dial for delicate cell culture work vs. fast media transfers
3. **Cordless Freedom:** Li-ion battery lasts all day — no cords tangling in the biosafety cabinet
4. **Autoclavable Nose Cone:** Silicone nose cone withstands 121°C sterilization — replace between users or protocols
5. **Hydrophobic Filter:** PTFE filter prevents liquid intrusion into the handpiece — protects electronics and prevents cross-contamination
6. **Universal Pipette Fit:** Holds any standard serological pipette from 1mL to 100mL

## Applications

- Cell culture media preparation and changing
- Aliquoting sterile reagents in biosafety cabinets
- Large-volume sample transfers (10–100mL)
- Tissue culture passaging
- Bacterial culture inoculation
- Blood and serum handling (clinical labs)

## Filter Replacement

The 0.45µm PTFE hydrophobic filter should be replaced:
- When aspiration speed noticeably decreases
- After liquid intrusion (visible moisture on filter)
- Monthly with heavy use
- Between users in shared labs

## Battery Care

- First use: Charge fully (3 hours)
- Daily use: Charge overnight
- Storage: Charge to 50% before long-term storage
- Replacement: Battery is user-replaceable
