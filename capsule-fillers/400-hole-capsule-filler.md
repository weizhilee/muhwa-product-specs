## Overview

A high-capacity 400-hole manual capsule filling machine designed for small commercial production and high-throughput laboratory use. Fills 400 capsules simultaneously — 4× the output of a standard 100-hole filler. ## Care Instructions

- Clean thoroughly between different powder formulations
- Dry completely before storage — moisture degrades ABS over time
- Store flat, not leaning — warped plates cause alignment issues
- Inspect hole alignment monthly during heavy use
